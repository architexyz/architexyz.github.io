  • COLOGNE ART TOWER

3D Animation in a hand-drawn style of Cologne Art Tower Project
for the World Architecture Festival 2025. Created on commission from 
TCHOBAN VOSS Architekten GmbH, Germany. The 85-meter-high listed Messeturm Köln (Cologne Trade Fair Tower) prominently opposite Cologne Cathedral on the other side of the Rhine. Built between 1922 and 1928, it is part of the historic Koelnmesse exhibition centre. 
The Messeturm is now to be repurposed to house a private art collection as a unique, vertical exhibition building
